# Create: MelatOwOin Wiki

Welcome to the **Create: MelatOwOin** wiki! :3

A chaos-themed Create addon for **Minecraft 1.20.1** on **Fabric and Forge**, inspired by [Melatonin(Create)](https://modrinth.com/mod/createmelatonin) by Alos2019.

Mod ID: `melatowoin`. Loader-abstracted with Architectury.
