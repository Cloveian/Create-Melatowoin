# Chloroform Chain

The chemistry pathway that takes you from common Minecraft ingredients to Chloroform, then onward to pills and sauces.

## The Chain

```
bone meal + apple + water   ──heated mix──►   Vinegar
egg        + Vinegar         ──heated mix──►   Calcium Acetate
Calcium Acetate              ──SUPERheat mix►  Acetone
Bleach + Acetone             ──mix──►          Chloroform + Water
```

For where Bleach comes from, see [[Furrycore-Chain]].

## Step Details

### 1. Vinegar

- **Machine:** Mixer (heated)
- **In:** 1× Bone Meal, 1× Apple, 20250 mb Water
- **Out:** 16200 mb Vinegar (fluid)

![Vinegar — Bone Meal + Apple + Water → Vinegar (heated mixer)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Cloveian/Create-Melatowoin/main/images/crafting/vinegar.png)

### 2. Calcium Acetate

- **Machine:** Mixer (heated)
- **In:** 1× Egg, 8100 mb Vinegar
- **Out:** 1× Calcium Acetate

![Calcium Acetate — Egg + Vinegar → Calcium Acetate (heated mixer)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Cloveian/Create-Melatowoin/main/images/crafting/calcium-acetate.png)

### 3. Acetone

- **Machine:** Mixer (**superheated** — Blaze Cake required on the Blaze Burner)
- **In:** 1× Calcium Acetate
- **Out:** 10125 mb Acetone (fluid)

![Acetone — Calcium Acetate → Acetone (superheated mixer)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Cloveian/Create-Melatowoin/main/images/crafting/acetone.png)

### 4. Chloroform

- **Machine:** Mixer (no heating required)
- **In:** 16200 mb Bleach + 20250 mb Acetone
- **Out:** 4050 mb Chloroform + 40500 mb Water (the leftover water can be reclaimed for more chemistry)

![Chloroform — Bleach + Acetone → Chloroform + Water (mixer, no heating)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Cloveian/Create-Melatowoin/main/images/crafting/chloroform.png)

## What You Use It For

| Use | Recipe |
|-----|--------|
| Cyan Pill | Compactor on 20250 mb Chloroform |
| Throwable Cyan Sauce | Compactor on 20250 mb Chloroform + Glass Bottle |

Then onward — see [[Pills-and-Throwables]] for the Orange Pill, Orange Sauce, and Orange Arrow.

## Tips for Automation

- The chloroform mixer outputs water as a byproduct — pipe it back to the vinegar mixer or the furrycore mixer to close the loop.
- Acetone needs superheat (Blaze Cake fuel). If you batch-produce acetone, store a buffer so the cake doesn't burn out mid-recipe.
- Calcium Acetate stacks to 16 — design around that limit if you're queueing recipes from a vault.
